Projects

  • Laparoscopic Probe for Abdominal Trauma: Developed a laparoscopic bioimpedance probe designed to improve the accuracy of margin assessment during laparoscopic surgeries...
  • 3D-Printed Arm Prostheses for LMICs: Designed affordable, functional arm prostheses for low- and middle-income countries (LMICs)...
  • Point-of-Care Cervical Cancer HPV Screening Device: Developed a low-cost, point-of-care device aimed at improving cervical cancer screening...
  • ENGS 31 UART Calculator Project: Developed a UART-based calculator using a Basys 3 board and a PS/2 keyboard...
  • ENGS 33 Bridge Design Project: Designed a truss bridge for a solid mechanics course to test structural efficiency...
  • Bottles, Bins, and Beyond (Recycling Contamination Reduction): Developed a recycling bin system to reduce contamination in beverage container recycling...
  • Nanotechnology for Water Filtration: Designed a nanotechnology-based water filtration system capable of removing pathogens and heavy metals...
  • NiFe Permanent Magnets for Electric Motors and Turbines: Investigated the properties of NiFe (Nickel-Iron) permanent magnets for electric motors and wind turbines...
  • Sweat-Based Glucose Monitor: Developed a non-invasive glucose monitoring device that uses sweat to measure glucose levels...
