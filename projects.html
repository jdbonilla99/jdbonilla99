Projects

3D-Printed Arm Prostheses for LMICs: Designed affordable, functional arm prostheses for low- and middle-income countries (LMICs), with a focus on regions such as Honduras. Using 3D printing technology and inexpensive materials like polypropylene and thermoplastics, the prostheses offer a customizable solution to individuals with arm amputations.

Laparoscopic Probe for Prostate Cancer Margin Detection: Developed a laparoscopic bioimpedance probe designed to detect positive surgical margins in prostate cancer patients during laparoscopic prostatectomy.

Gold Nanoparticle-Enhanced Radiation Therapy for Cancer: Developed a novel treatment for pancreatic cancer using gold nanoparticles functionalized with P1G4 antibodies.

ENGS 33 Bridge Design Project: Designed a truss bridge as part of a solid mechanics course to test structural efficiency and material usage.

ENGS 31 UART Calculator Project: This project involved the development of a UART-based calculator using a Basys 3 board and a PS/2 keyboard.

Point-of-Care Cervical Cancer HPV Screening Device: Developed a low-cost, point-of-care device aimed at improving cervical cancer screening, particularly in low-resource settings.

Bottles, Bins, and Beyond (Recycling Contamination Reduction): Developed a recycling bin system aimed at reducing contamination in beverage container recycling.

Sweat-Based Glucose Monitor: Developed a non-invasive glucose monitoring device that uses sweat as a medium to measure glucose levels, eliminating the need for blood samples.

NiFe Permanent Magnets for Electric Motors and Turbines: Investigated the properties of NiFe (Nickel-Iron) permanent magnets for potential applications in electric motors and wind turbines.
